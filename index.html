What is Céu?

Céu is a reactive language targeted at embedded systems and intended to offer a higher-level and safer alternative to C. Check out our interactive tutorial to learn more.

Céu looks like this

loop do
    par/and do
      await 100ms;
      _printf("Hello ");
    with
      await 250ms;
      _printf("World!\n");
    end
end
